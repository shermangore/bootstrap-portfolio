I've been a professional software developer since 1997 when I successfully created management software for one of the largest deli companies in the United States in 1997. Since that first job, I've worked for international companies, local companies, and owned my own consulting business with clients in the United States, Ireland, and Canada - including household names like Wells Fargo and Bombardier.

My current technology stack is the Microsoft stack with a singular focus on the .NET Framework, mostly with C#. I have over a year of experience with MVC and Entity Framework Core. I'm a strong proponent of code-first development, but I have extensive experience in both code-first as well as data-first development.

Recently became a student at The Coding Bootcamp at UCLA in an effort to get a solid, in-depth foundation in the MERN stack (MongoDB, Ember.js, React.js, and Node.js). I plan to use this new foundation to start a new career trajectory that leads me to exciting and fulfulling opportunities in the near future.
